Greetings · Introductions · Farewells

Sarah meets Rawan, one of her students, and her friend in the street.

Good morning, Rawan. - Sarah

Rawan - Good morning, Sarah. How are you?

I'm fine thanks, and you? - Sarah

Rawan - Not too bad. Sarah, this is my friend Nora, Nora this is Sarah my English teacher.

Nora - Pleased to meet you.

Pleased to meet you too. Are you from Germany, Nora? - Sarah

Nora - Yes, East Germany, from Dresden. And you, are you from London?

No, I'm from Derby, but I live in London now. - Sarah

Rawan - Well, good bye Sarah, it was nice to see you.

Yes, good bye. - Sarah
